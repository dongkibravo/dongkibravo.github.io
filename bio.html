Biography


Dong Park (full name: Donghwi Park) was born in March 3rd, 1992 in Honolulu, HI. He has moved around some cities; Honolulu HI, Daegu S.Korea, San Antonio TX, Austin TX. Currently he lives in Medford, Massachusetts and attend as a full-time student at Tufts University.

He moved to South Korea when he was three years old and spent most of his childhood there until he moved back to the states when he was 15years old. In San Antonio, he started going to Warren High School and Moved to Austin and graduated from Westlake High School in 2011. During high school, he played tennis for the high school team for four years.

He loves doing and watching every kinds of sports but cricket. He has been snowboarding since 2012-2013 season. He is a competitive N64 version Super Smash Bros player with his main character Kirby. Also he enjoys playing computer games like Civilization series and League of Legend.

He follows the professional tennis league, the Korean professional baseball league, and the National Football League. His favorite teams and players are Roger Federer, Andy Roddick, Samsung Lions, and Dallas Cowboys.
